    Skittles and Their Creator

    The original creator of Skittles is unknown. Some sources state that is was the Wrigley Company, the company who still produce Skittles today was the company that created Skittles. While other sources state that it was an unknown British Company who originally created them and would later pass on Skittles to the Wrigely Company.

    Skittles to be Enjoyed by Anyone

    Up until 2009 Skittles made use of an animal-derived gelatin to make Skittles. In 2009 production of Skittles using animal-derived gelatin was ceased, making consumption of KSittles suitable for vegans, vegetarians and some religious groups
